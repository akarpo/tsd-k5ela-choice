# 10 — Citations and appendix

**Date:** 2026-04
**Asked for:** Tighten the citation scheme so every claim on a content slide resolves to an appendix entry.

## Prompt(s) quoted
> Every number on a slide needs to resolve to a citation in the appendix.

## What was produced
- Audit pass: each on-deck quantitative claim → assigned a numbered marker.
- Appendix entries written for each marker (data source, year, URL or DOI where applicable).
- Slides where a claim couldn't be cited cleanly were rewritten or dropped.

## Why this mattered
Forcing every claim to cite cleanly killed several rhetorical shortcuts and forced the underlying data work to be tighter — a few claims disappeared because no clean source existed for them, which is the right outcome.
