# 17 — Grade-level hypothesis

**Date:** 2026-04
**Asked for:** Test the steelman of Balanced Literacy — "maybe BL hurts early grades but helps later grades" — across the 50-district universe.

## Prompt(s) quoted
> Test the BL-helps-for-later-grades hypothesis. If it's true the deck has to say so.

## What was produced
- Grade-level (G3 / G4 / G5) Pre→Post-COVID Δ for SoR-recent vs BL-stayed districts.
- Slide 18 with the grade-level table.
- Finding: SoR-recent districts gained at G3 (+0.031) — *did not* decline; BL districts declined across all three grades.

## Why this mattered
Honest analysis means testing the strongest version of the opposing view. If BL had helped at later grades, the deck would have said so. It didn't — and that's a more defensible conclusion than just citing the All-students number.
