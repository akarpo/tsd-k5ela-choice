# 02 — Education Scorecard "Districts on the Rise"

**Date:** 2026-04
**Asked for:** Identify the K-5 ELA curricula used by each of the 20 districts named in Education Scorecard's 2026 "Districts on the Rise," so we can see whether the gainers cluster around any particular curriculum.

## Prompt(s) quoted
> Pull the curricula used by each of the Education Scorecard "Districts on the Rise" — I want to know if there's a pattern.

## What was produced
- Curriculum lookup for all 20 Districts on the Rise (SoR vs BL classification).
- Cross-tab that became part of slide 6 (tier columns) and slide 18 (grade-level reading).
- Note that several "rising" districts use SoR-aligned curricula but a few don't, which became the SoR-doesn't-guarantee-recovery caveat later in the deck.

## Why this mattered
Education Scorecard's gain methodology is demographic-adjusted, which is the closest public proxy to "doing better than expected." Pinning curricula to those districts gave us a second evidence layer beyond raw SEDA deltas, and the *not all gainers are SoR* finding kept the deck honest.
