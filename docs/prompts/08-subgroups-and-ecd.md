# 08 — Subgroups and ECD

**Date:** 2026-04
**Asked for:** Break out the deltas by racial subgroup (Asian / White / Black / Hispanic) and by economic disadvantage (ECD / Not-ECD), not just All.

## Prompt(s) quoted
> Break the deltas out by subgroup — All hides the disparity story.

## What was produced
- Subgroup-level Pre→Post-COVID Δ for each of the 50 districts.
- Slide showing Troy's subgroup deltas side-by-side with the recovery cohort (this became slide 16).
- Hispanic subgroup flagged as small-N for Troy → footnote added on slide 4.

## Why this mattered
The All-students number can hide a subgroup collapsing. Looking at ECD and racial subgroups is what surfaced that Troy's gap widened post-COVID for some groups — which is exactly the kind of finding parents and board members will ask about.
