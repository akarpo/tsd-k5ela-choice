# 04 — Sustained outperformers (Steubenville et al.)

**Date:** 2026-04
**Asked for:** Add a layer of evidence built on districts that have *sustained* high ELA performance across years — Steubenville being the canonical example — rather than only districts that recently recovered.

## Prompt(s) quoted
> Include Steubenville and other sustained outperformers — I want a separate evidence layer for districts that have stayed high, not just bounced back.

## What was produced
- New peer category: "sustained outperformers" (multi-year ≥ +0.3 cs above predicted).
- Slides showing their curricula and demographics next to the recovery cohort.
- Reinforced the SoR-association narrative — most sustained outperformers also use SoR-aligned core curricula.

## Why this mattered
"Recovery" is a fragile signal — a district can post a big Pre→Post-COVID Δ because it cratered hardest and then snapped back. Sustained outperformance is more durable evidence. Layering the two together is what made the deck's curriculum claim non-trivial.
