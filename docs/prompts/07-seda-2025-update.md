# 07 — SEDA 2025.1 update

**Date:** 2026-04
**Asked for:** Switch from older SEDA releases to SEDA 2025.1, which covers 2009–2025 (the first release with credible post-COVID years).

## Prompt(s) quoted
> Use SEDA 2025.1 — it's the first release that covers post-COVID with enough years to compute a stable Δ.

## What was produced
- Data ingest pointed at SEDA 2025.1 cohort-standardized (cs) scale files.
- Pre/Post-COVID windows defined as 2017–2019 mean vs 2022–2025 mean.

## Why this mattered
Pre-2025 SEDA didn't have enough post-COVID years to compute a stable post-window mean. The 2025.1 release made the headline Pre→Post Δ defensible, which is the central comparison the deck rests on.
