# 15 — Jargon cleanup

**Date:** 2026-04
**Asked for:** Strip made-up or invented jargon from the deck. Phrases that sounded technical but had no concrete definition.

## Prompt(s) quoted
> Stop using made-up phrases like "home compensation strained." Either it means something specific or it goes.

## What was produced
- Pass through every slide flagging undefined phrases.
- Replacements either (a) cited a real construct (e.g. SEDA cs scale) or (b) rewrote the sentence in plain language.
- Several callouts deleted entirely because they didn't survive translation.

## Why this mattered
Invented jargon disguises hand-waving as analysis. Forcing every term to either resolve to a real construct or get rewritten is what kept the deck defensible under a hostile read.
