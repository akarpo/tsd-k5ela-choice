# 05 — MDE-list curriculum comparison

**Date:** 2026-04
**Asked for:** Add slides comparing districts that use curricula appearing on Michigan Department of Education's reviewed/approved curriculum list.

## Prompt(s) quoted
> Add slides comparing the districts that use MDE-list curricula — Troy's selection has to defend itself against that list, not against an arbitrary peer set.

## What was produced
- Subset slice of the peer universe restricted to districts using MDE-list curricula.
- Comparison of SEDA Δ within that subset.
- Reframed the recommendation around MDE-vetted options rather than the open universe.

## Why this mattered
A Michigan school district can be challenged on *why this particular curriculum* — the MDE list is the most defensible answer. Anchoring the comparison to that list pre-empted "you didn't even look at the approved options" pushback.
